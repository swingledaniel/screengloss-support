ScreenGloss

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Overview

ScreenGloss processes a screenshot only when you choose to send it to the app, usually through an iOS Shortcut. It recognizes text, shows selectable text boxes, and can translate that text on your device.

Data Collection

ScreenGloss does not collect data from you. The app has no account system, analytics, advertising, or tracking. It does not send your screenshots or recognized text to the developer or to a developer-operated server.

On-Device Processing

Text recognition runs with on-device machine learning. Translation uses Apple's on-device Translation framework. iOS may download a language model the first time you translate a language, but ScreenGloss does not transmit the screenshot or recognized text to the developer.

Optional Pleco Action

If you tap the Pleco action, ScreenGloss asks iOS to open the Pleco app with the text you selected. This happens only after your explicit action. Pleco's handling of that text is governed by Pleco's own privacy policy.

Storage

ScreenGloss stores preferences such as your output language and overlay settings on your device. It does not maintain a history of your screenshots or recognized text.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, please use the support link on the ScreenGloss support page.
